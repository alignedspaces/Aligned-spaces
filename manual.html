Official Cleaner Manual & SOP

Our Commitment to You

Welcome to Aligned Spaces. We know that behind every fresh and beautiful home, there is a hard-working, dedicated professional. You are the face, the heart, and the hands of this company. I cannot be in every house at once, which means my trust is placed entirely in you.

In the cleaning industry, many companies treat their staff as just numbers. Here, we do things differently. We believe that happy, valued employees create happy, loyal clients. You will always be treated with the utmost respect, fairness, and support by management. We have your back, and we want you to feel proud to wear our uniform.

In return, we ask for one thing: Excellence. We have zero tolerance for shortcuts because our clients pay for a premium, 5-star experience. We hold you to the highest standard, knowing that you are fully capable of achieving it. Take pride in your work, follow this manual meticulously, and let's grow together.

💎 Core Values & Professionalism

  • Punctuality: Always arrive exactly at the time specified on your Job Sheet. If you are running late, notify management immediately.
  • Appearance & Uniform: You must wear your official Aligned Spaces embroidered scrubs (3 sets are provided free upon hire; additional sets can be purchased at cost). For safety and professionalism, closed-toe, non-slip tennis shoes are mandatory. Crocs, sandals, and open-toed shoes are strictly prohibited (they are a slip hazard).
  • 0 JUDGMENT POLICY: Many homes will be difficult to clean. Remember that we all carry internal chaos at times, and this often reflects in our personal spaces. We never know if behind the dust and accumulated grease there is an overwhelmed mother, a person battling depression, or someone grieving the loss of a loved one. We ask for your utmost empathy, absolute discretion, and compassion. We are here to restore their space and bring them peace, not to judge them.
  • Respect the Space: Treat every home like a luxury hotel. Do not move expensive items unless necessary, and always put things exactly back where they were.
  • Team Culture & Communication: Clear communication is essential—not just with management, but with your coworkers. Always ask, never assume. We cannot read each other's minds, so asking questions is the best way to prevent misunderstandings. Organize the workload with your partners so you can work together efficiently and pleasantly. We spend a third of our day at work, and this job is physically and mentally demanding. Getting along and supporting each other is vital to making our days enjoyable. Respect is absolutely non-negotiable. We have a zero-tolerance policy for gossip or fighting; any toxic behavior will result in immediate probation or automatic termination. Be kind, be helpful, and protect our team culture.
  • Job Sheet Communication: Always read both the Client Instructions and the Manager Notes on your Job Sheet before starting the service.
  • Client Privacy & Security: Total confidentiality is required. You may never take photos or videos of the inside of a client's home without explicit management permission. Do not post anything regarding our clients on your personal social media. When leaving a property, you must physically double-check that all doors and windows are securely locked.
  • Damage & Honesty Policy: Accidents happen. If you accidentally break a vase, scratch a surface, or damage an appliance, report it to management immediately. Honest mistakes are understandable, but hiding them is unacceptable and may result in suspension. Transparency is the foundation of our trust.
  • Mobile Phone & Headphone Policy: You may listen to music or podcasts using only one earbud so you remain aware of your surroundings and can hear your partner or the client. Personal phone calls on speakerphone while cleaning are strictly prohibited. We understand many of you are parents; if you must take or make a quick emergency call, you do not have to leave the house, but we ask for your utmost discretion and a lowered voice. If the client is speaking to you, immediately remove your earbud to give them your full attention.
  • Quality Control & Recleans: We operate with perfectionist standards. If a client complains or requests a reclean, do not take it personally. Use the feedback gracefully to improve your technique. Our goal is 5-star excellence every single time, and constructive feedback helps us achieve that.

📋 Comprehensive Checklists by Service

1. Signature Clean (Standard Maintenance)

The routine maintenance clean to keep the home looking fresh and detailed.

ALL ROOMS

  • Dusting and wiping top-to-bottom (ceiling fans, vents, blinds, baseboards)
  • Wiping down all accessible surfaces and furniture
  • Vacuuming carpets and mopping hard floors
  • Emptying all trash receptacles
  • Sanitizing high-touch points (doorknobs, light switches)
  • Sliding glass doors cleaned (inside & out)

KITCHEN

  • Wiping exterior of all cabinets
  • Cleaning exterior and interior of microwave
  • Degreasing and scrubbing stovetop
  • Sanitizing countertops and backsplashes
  • Polishing the sink and fixtures
  • Wiping down the exterior of all appliances

BATHROOMS

  • Deep scrubbing of showers, tubs, and tile grout
  • Thorough sanitizing of toilets (inside and out)
  • Polishing mirrors and glass doors
  • Cleaning and polishing sink fixtures
  • Folding towels hotel-style

2. Transition Reset (Deep Clean / Move-in & Move-out)

The most intensive clean for empty homes or yearly deep-cleans.

ALL ROOMS

  • Dusting and wiping top-to-bottom (ceiling fans, vents, blinds, baseboards)
  • Vacuuming and mopping all floors
  • Sliding glass doors cleaned (inside & out)
  • Thorough removal of cobwebs in all corners and ceilings
  • Detailed dusting of all blinds and shutters

DEEP INTERIORS

  • Thorough cleaning inside all empty kitchen cabinets and drawers
  • Thorough cleaning inside all empty bathroom cabinets and drawers
  • Wiping down shelves inside all empty closets throughout the house

KITCHEN & BATHROOMS

  • Deep scrubbing of all bathrooms and kitchen surfaces
  • Deep cleaning the interior of the refrigerator and freezer
  • Deep cleaning the interior of the oven

DETAILS & EDGES

  • Intensive hand-wiping of all baseboards
  • Wiping down door frames and window sills

3. 5-Star Turnover (Airbnb / Short Term Rentals)

Fast, precise, and focused on presentation for the next guest.

THE BASELINE CLEAN

  • Thorough dusting of all surfaces and furniture
  • Vacuuming carpets and mopping hard floors
  • Emptying all trash receptacles
  • Sanitizing high-touch points (remotes, doorknobs, lockboxes)
  • Deep scrubbing of bathrooms (toilets, showers, sinks)
  • Cleaning kitchen surfaces, inside microwave, and stovetop
  • Sliding glass doors cleaned (inside & out)

STAGING & RESET

  • Stripping used linens and towels
  • Washing and changing all bed linens
  • Making beds with professional hotel-style tucks
  • Folding fresh towels aesthetically
  • Resetting decorative pillows, throws, and furniture placement

INVENTORY & RESTOCKING

  • Restocking toilet paper and paper towels
  • Refilling hand soaps, shampoo, and body wash dispensers
  • Restocking coffee, tea, and basic kitchen supplies
  • Emptying the dishwasher and putting away clean dishes

DAMAGE CHECK & REPORTING

  • Visual inspection for stains, damages, or missing items
  • Immediate notification to the host of any issues found
  • Checking that appliances and lights are turned off
  • Securing all windows and doors before departure

INCLUDED EXTRAS

  • IN FRIDGE, IN OVEN, IN EMPTY CABINETS and balcony and patio set is included

4. Post-Construction

Removing fine dust after renovations. Requires extreme attention to detail.

DUST ERADICATION

  • Dry dusting walls and ceilings to remove fine drywall dust
  • Vacuuming inside and outside of all vents and duct covers
  • Wiping down all ceiling fans and light fixtures
  • Detailed dusting of all baseboards, door frames, and window sills
  • Thorough vacuuming of all carpets and hard floors (multiple passes)
  • Sliding glass doors cleaned (inside & out)

DEEP CABINETRY CLEANING

  • Vacuuming and wet-wiping the inside of all new kitchen cabinets and drawers
  • Vacuuming and wet-wiping the inside of all new bathroom cabinets and vanities
  • Wiping down the exterior of all cabinetry to remove sawdust and fingerprints
  • Cleaning all newly installed closet shelving

SURFACE RESTORATION & POLISHING

  • Removing small paint splatters, adhesive, or caulk residue
  • Polishing all new appliances
  • Deep scrubbing of newly installed tiles to remove grout haze
  • Polishing all mirrors, interior glass doors, and interior windows
  • Shining all new chrome, brass, and stainless steel fixtures

5. Tailored Focus

Custom cleaning based on an hourly rate. The client sets the rules.

CUSTOM CHECKLIST

  • Focus entirely on deep cleaning the kitchen and organizing the pantry
  • Spend the hours scrubbing grout and polishing all bathrooms
  • Help with intensive decluttering or seasonal closet organization
  • Tackle specific, high-traffic areas before a party or event
  • Handle unpacking and breaking down boxes in a new home

🛡️ Health, Safety & Daily Operations

  • Health & Safety (Your well-being is our priority): Never mix cleaning chemicals, especially bleach and ammonia, as they create toxic gases. For your safety, we do NOT clean severe biohazards (human/pet waste, blood, extensive black mold, or severe infestations). If you encounter these, take a photo, leave the immediate area, and contact management. Always use proper lifting techniques and avoid climbing higher than a two-step stool. Your safety always comes first.
  • Pet Protocol: We love our clients' furry friends, but safety is paramount. Always ensure doors and gates are firmly closed so pets do not escape. If a pet shows signs of aggression or you feel unsafe, step out of the room, close the door, and notify management immediately. We will never force you to work in an environment where you feel threatened by an animal.
  • Time Tracking & Reporting: Communication of your progress is crucial. You must clock in exactly when you arrive and clock out when you leave. For turnovers and deep cleans, submit 'before and after' photos to management. This protects you by proving the quality of your hard work and helps us maintain our 5-star standard.
  • Equipment & Supply Care: Our tools are an investment in your success. Empty your vacuum canister at the end of every day and wipe it down. Keep cleaning cloths separated (do not mix greasy kitchen rags with glass microfiber cloths). Make sure all liquid bottles are tightly closed to prevent spills. A professional always cares for their tools.
  • Tipping & Gifts: You work incredibly hard, and clients may want to reward that! You are absolutely allowed to accept cash tips and gifts from clients. A simple, gracious "Thank you" is perfect. We only ask that you never solicit or ask for tips. If a client leaves cash out, do not assume it is a tip unless there is a note specifically saying so.

🚨 Emergency Protocols

Accidental Damage: Take a picture immediately, clean it up if safe, and text management. Do NOT hide it.

Client is Home: Be polite, respectful, and keep noise to a minimum.

Locked Out: Double-check the code on your Job Sheet. If it fails, call management. Do NOT ring the doorbell multiple times.
