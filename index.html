Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium services.

What is included in the Signature Clean?
Our Signature Clean is designed to maintain your home at its highest alignment. It includes thorough dusting, wiping down of all surfaces, vacuuming, mopping, and comprehensive kitchen and bathroom sanitation. We bring all of our own premium, eco-friendly supplies.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Not at all. You do not need to be present for the cleaning. You can simply provide us with an access code or tell us where the key is located. Our team operates with the utmost discretion and care, so you can return to a perfectly refreshed space.
How does the booking and payment process work?
We value transparency. You can generate an instant quote on our website, select your preferred time, and secure your booking with a 20% deposit. The remaining balance is handled only after the service is completed to your satisfaction.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. ALIGNED SPACES holds active business licenses with both the State of Nevada and the local City. We are also fully insured and bonded. Your peace of mind and the safety of your property are our highest priorities.
What if I have pets in my home?
We love pets! Our team is trained to be mindful of your furry friends and we carefully monitor our cleaning products to keep them out of reach of curious paws. Just let us know in your booking notes if there are any special instructions for them while we clean.
Do I need to provide any cleaning supplies?
No, you don't need to provide a thing. We arrive fully equipped with our own curated selection of premium, eco-friendly supplies and professional-grade equipment.
